About Me

My name is Tahaka Gabrio Albert, a passionate student in Software Engineering at the University of Technology, Mauritius. I am currently developing The Underground Republic, a project that merges web design, storytelling, and my deep fascination for ants.

My passion for ants started at a young age, observing their coordination and complex societies. Through this website, I aim to share the beauty, intelligence, and inspiration we can draw from these extraordinary creatures.
